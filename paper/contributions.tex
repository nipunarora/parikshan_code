The impact of sandbox testing can be seen in several different ways

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Sandbox Live Testing}

  \item \textbf{Fault Tolerance}
  \item \textbf{Verification}
  \item \textbf{Integration Testing}
\end{itemize}
